Interpolating Control Toolbox

The Interpolating Control Toolbox (or ICT for short) is an open source, Matlab-based toolbox for the constrained control of discrete-time linear systems.

Authors

ICT has been developed by Sheila Scialanga (ex-PhD student at the University of Glasgow; now with MathWorks, Cambridge) and Konstantinos Ampountolas, University of Glasgow, UK.
